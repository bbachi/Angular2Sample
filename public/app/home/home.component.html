Sponsor

Ravecrate allows you to search for possible sponsors for your events and gain leads. At the same time, users can sponsor events and make it public through Ravecrate.

Host

With our mobile app and website, you can host events and get paid for it. In addition, you can hire freelancers and gain PR, or even persuade sponsors to lower the cost of an event.

Attendees

While Ravecrate takes the hassle out of event planning, it also makes reaching out to your guests even easier. Don’t dial up all your friends individually and text each of them the time, date, and location when you can send out mass invites through our mobile app or website. This feature works the other way around as well: guests have access to event information at their fingertips, making it easier for them to find you.

Feedback

Don’t hesitate to tell us about your experience with Ravecrate. We review all feedback and implement changes in real time to make Ravecrate the best it can be. We understand the importance of catering to the diverse needs of event planners, freelancers, and guests to make events as successful as possible.

Freelancer

The Ravecrate community is home to a number of freelancers who are willing to help you pull off your event successfully. You don’t have to look for ads in the paper or scroll through online listings; freelancers, their skills, and locations are made available directly through Ravecrate.

Proximity

Instantly discover the location of nearby parties, events, and freelancers with our mobile app’s proximity feature. Ravecrate allows you to view interests you have in common with people close by, including their likes and past events they have attended. You can also customize your searches, whether you’re looking for dedicated developers or generous investors for you next enterprise.
